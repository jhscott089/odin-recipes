Omelette

Description

My homemade omelette recipe.

Ingredients include:

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Stir eggs in with some butter and salt
  2. Add more butter to a pan, cook until slightly browned (do not overcook, or the butter will burn)
  3. Add eggs to pan, swirl around
  4. Once edges are crispy, fold in thirds
