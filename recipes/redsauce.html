Red Sauce

Description

My homemade red sauce recipe using canned tomatoes, onion, garlic, and herbs.

Ingredients include:

  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Oregeno
  • Red chili flakes
  • Basil
  • Thyme
  • Parmigiano Reggiano
  • (Optional) Olives

Steps

  1. Saute onions in liive oil until slightly browned
  2. Add garlic and chili flakes, saute for no more than a minute
  3. Add tomato sauce and stir
  4. Add seasonings (Basil, oregeno, thyme)
  5. Cook on medium low for 30 minutes
  6. Stir in parmigiano and serve with pasta, you may add liives over the top
